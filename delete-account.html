Delete Your Account

If you want your account removed, please follow the instructions below.

Important: The developer contact listed on the Play Store / Apple App Store is the official channel to request account deletion.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email from the address registered with your myjinalay account to the developer email listed on the app's Play Store / Apple App Store page.
  2. Include in the email:
    • Your registered email address and/or phone number
    • Subject line: Request: Delete my myjinalay account
    • Optional: short reason for deletion
  3. The dev team will verify your request and confirm processing.

What will be removed

Upon verification, personal identifiers (email, phone) associated with your account will be removed. Transactional or financial records may be retained as required for legal, tax, or accounting purposes â€” these records will be anonymized where possible.

Processing time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days after verification. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

Questions

For questions about the deletion process, contact the developer email shown on the Play Store / Apple App Store listing.
